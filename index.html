How often do the lotteries run ? +

Every Sunday at 12pm EST, the prior week’s contest will close and the new one will open for entry. Each contest runs for one week.

Where do you ship ?+

Shipping is limited to North America for now. That may change in the future.

Are the shoes real ?+

Yes, all sneakers are 100% authentic.

How is the winner chosen ? +

Every ticket purchased gives you another entry into our lottery system. We use a system to randomly generate a winning ticket. The draw will be broadcasted live on our Instagram.

You’ll have 24 hours to reply to the winning announcement email with your shipping address. If you do not reply within that timeframe, we will choose another winner.

We’ve built an extensive network of online and brick-and-mortar retailers who act as our suppliers. Before featuring a shoe, we ensure that all US Men’s Size 6-13 are attainable.

What happens if I win ?+

Winners will be contacted on Sunday by email. If your ticket is chosen, you’ll have 24 hours to reply with your shipping address.
